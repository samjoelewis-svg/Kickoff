How to play
A mystery football match photo drops every day.

Your job: guess the two teams and the year.

Type a team name in the Home or Away box, and the year in the Year box. Hit Submit.

Each wrong guess zooms the photo out a bit more and unlocks a clue. Correct answers lock in green and don't cost you a guess.

Score up to 3 points — one per team, one for the year.

Copy your score and paste it to your group chat.
Year formats: 1998 · 1997-98 · 97-98
